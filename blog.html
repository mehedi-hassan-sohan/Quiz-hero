2. what is difference between local storage session storage and cookies

Answer: local storage and session storage are designed to store data on the client-side without the need for communication with the server, while cookies are used to store data on the client-side and are sent to the server with every request. Local storage has the largest storage capacity, while session storage has a shorter lifespan, and cookies have the smallest storage capacity but can be accessed by both client and server-side scripts.
